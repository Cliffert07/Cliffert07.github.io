How To Use Instagram Reels To Grow Your Business

Gedlin Frelin (CEO & Founder)

January 13, 2021

Next

Instagram Reels are a great way to engage with the current Instagram community and even reach a wider audience.

Here are some ways to use Reels for growing your business:

1. Give Followers A Peek Behind The Scenes

Your followers will always love to see the face behind the business. People will have more trust in your business if you tell them about what really drives the business and about what keeps it up and running day after day.

2. Educate Potential Customers

You can certainly use Instagram Reels to your advantage here. Share valuable and insightful information that can help potential customers make a buying decision in your brand’s favour.

3. Make It Fun And Personal

While you are promoting your business, no one said that you cannot make this process fun and personal. It doesn’t have to be all about your next sales or current promotions, it can also be about the audience that feels a personal connection to you too.

4. Share tips and tricks

There’s nothing that screams “Hey, look at this” more than useful tips & tricks. People adore learning about new trends, hacks and tips that can make their lives easier. Carve some of the time you invest in Reels to share tips and tricks, fun facts and helpful information with your audience.

5. Leverage Q&As in Your Instagram Reels.

When it comes to thinking up content ideas, nothing beats the brainpower of your community. Entertain your audience with daily or weekly Q&A sessions and don’t be afraid to add some humour or interesting visuals.
