Questioning how to increase Instagram engagement?

As brands you need to look further into performance metrics and track comments, saves, shares, Instagram Stories views, and even DMs to understand how they are performing.

1. Spend some time

Monitoring and tracking your Instagram analytics to learn when the best time to post is for your audience or use meta business suites it helps you suggest the best time for a post or story.

2. Experiments

Trial, error, and content experiments are key to being ahead of the curve and having a stronger strategy.

Using carousel posts to share valuable tips and information can lead to more saves, as your audience can refer back to the post when they want to act on the tips.
